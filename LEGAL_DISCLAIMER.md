# Legal Disclaimer & Acceptable Use

## 1. Nature of the Project

`venture-ops` is a collection of Markdown prompts, Node.js scripts, and HTML templates. It is strictly a local execution tool. The maintainers do not host, deploy, or operate an AI system, nor do they provide API access to large language models.

Users download the code, run it on their own machines, and connect it to their own AI provider. The maintainers have no visibility into, control over, or responsibility for how the tool is used after download.

## 2. Data Privacy

The maintainers do not act as a Data Controller or Data Processor for founder data you place into the tool.

- Founder details, investor notes, and fundraising materials are processed locally on your machine unless you choose to send them to an AI provider.
- When you use an AI CLI tool, your data is sent directly to the provider you chose. Review that provider's privacy terms.
- The project does not collect analytics, telemetry, or usage data.
- API keys, credentials, and private fundraising files are gitignored by default. Never commit them to a public repository.

## 3. AI Model Behavior

This tool interfaces with AI models via third-party tools. The maintainers do not control those models and cannot guarantee their behavior.

- **Hallucinations:** AI models may fabricate investor facts, deadlines, partner names, market data, or startup claims. You must verify all generated materials before sending them.
- **Safety guardrails:** The default prompts instruct the AI not to submit applications or send outreach on your behalf. If you override those instructions, you accept full responsibility for the AI's actions.
- **Evaluation accuracy:** Fit scores and recommendations are heuristic outputs, not guaranteed truth. Use them to inform judgment, not replace it.

## 4. Third-Party Platforms

Venture-Ops may interact with investor websites, accelerator programs, grant pages, startup websites, and other third-party sources.

- Users must comply with the terms of service of every platform they interact with.
- Do not use this tool to spam investors, mass-submit generic applications, or misrepresent traction.
- Any consequences from platform or program violations are solely the responsibility of the user.

## 5. Acceptable Use

Acceptable use includes:

- evaluating investor and accelerator fit
- generating draft decks, one-pagers, memos, and application answers
- maintaining a private fundraising tracker
- monitoring relevant market and category news

Unacceptable use includes:

- auto-submitting applications or sending outreach without human review
- fabricating traction, customer proof, investor relationships, or financial claims
- using the tool to deceive, harass, or impersonate others
