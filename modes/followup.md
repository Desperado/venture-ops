# Follow-Up Mode

Use `data/targets.md` and `data/follow-ups.md` to identify follow-up opportunities.

Cadence defaults:
- contacted: follow up after 5-7 business days
- meeting: follow up within 24 hours with recap and next step
- diligence: follow up every 3-5 business days if waiting on them
- accelerator application: follow the posted timeline; do not invent urgency

Draft follow-ups only. The founder sends them.
